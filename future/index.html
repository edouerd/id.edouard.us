Future Works

In the most sincere manner, I deeply wish for an all–around better, more futuristic, more egalitarian, more ecologically-friendly world.

In approaching problems that span from the smallest to the most massive scales, and involve tackling deep systems–problems that no singular “design” alone can possibly fix, I remain optimistic that there is truly good work left to be done — work that will actually leave everyone and the planet(s!) better off.

Larger–scale works I think about all the time:

  • Physical–digital product systems for colonists on Mars
  • Building a Network of the Dead, a dynamic relation–construction of dead folks
  • Open source learning (self-actualization) networks

Subjects I’ve recently been curious about, and have begun to seriously research:

  • New hegemonic formats of communication, “The New American Interface”
  • Virtual reality web browsing, or how we can break the glowing rectangle
  • Manufacturing methodologies, and how they can be easily communicated

I’m tinkering with these things:

  • Computerless Website Creation
  • Physical affordance recontextualisation, imbuing simple objects with prior memories
