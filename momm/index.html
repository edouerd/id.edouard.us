Methods of Mass Manufacture

Methods of Mass Manufacture (MoMM) is an open curriculum I'm developing under the Learning Gardens initative. The overarching goal of this study is to convey a complete understanding of how industrially-produced objects are defined, produced, and assembled across globally-spanning supply chains.

My hope is that with a better understanding of 1.) product design at scale, 2.) high-level materials science, and 3.) manufacturing methodologies, anyone passing through this learning material will gain a better understanding of the mass-produced world around them.

While no formal syllabus has been assembled thus far, I'm constantly updating the materials I have within MoMM's Github repository. If you'd like to contribute towards this project, or help out in any way, feel free to send issues and pull requests my way. :^)
