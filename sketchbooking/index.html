Sketchbooking

As time presses forward, it's fun to revisit the many scribbled-in sketchbooks and looseleaf papers I've collected over the years and see old ideas and notes that might have been worth recording. Everyone who draws in a serious manner has their own philosophy as to why they draw the way they do, or why the draw in the first place,
